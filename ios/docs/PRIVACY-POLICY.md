# Prismet — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 29, 2026_

Prismet ("the app", "we", "us") is a free collection of classic games. This
policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We keep it short because
we collect as little as possible.

## What we collect

- **Phone number** — used only to sign you in. When you enter your number, our
  authentication provider sends a one-time code by SMS to verify it's you. We store
  your number to recognize your account on future sign-ins.
- **Profile** — a display name and an avatar (emoji + color) you choose. This is
  shown to you, and (in future social features) to friends you connect with.
- **Game activity** — your scores and progress. Today these are stored on your
  device and in your account so they follow you between sessions.
- **Advertising data** — the app shows a banner ad through Google AdMob. Google may
  collect a device advertising identifier and limited device/usage information to
  serve and measure ads. See Google's policies below.

We do **not** collect your contacts, location, photos, or microphone, and we do
**not** sell your personal information.

## Service providers

- **Supabase** — stores your account, profile, and scores. (supabase.com/privacy)
- **Twilio** — delivers the SMS verification code. (twilio.com/legal/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** — serves the in-app banner ad. (policies.google.com/privacy and
  support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543 for partner data use)

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy terms.

## How we use it

- To sign you in and keep you signed in.
- To show your profile and save your scores.
- To display a single banner advertisement that helps keep the app free.

## Your choices and rights

- **Limit ad tracking** — you can reset or limit your advertising identifier in
  iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and → Apple Advertising.
- **Delete your account** — contact us (below) and we will delete your account,
  profile, and associated data. You can also stop using the app at any time.

## Children

Prismet is rated 10+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not
directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children
under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will
remove it.

## Data retention

We keep your account data while your account exists. When you delete your account,
we delete your profile and scores. Verification codes are short-lived and not
retained after sign-in.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by
the "Last updated" date above.
